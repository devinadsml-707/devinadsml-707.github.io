Who I Am

I'm an Environmental Engineer and Data Scientist with a background that spans two continents and multiple industries. After completing my MSc in Environmental Sciences at Wageningen University & Research (Netherlands) and a BSc in Environmental Engineering at ITB, I've worked across coal mining, aquaculture, and environmental consulting — building deep expertise in wastewater systems, AMDAL compliance, and ESG reporting.

What sets me apart is the rare combination of genuine domain knowledge and modern data science capability. I understand the data I'm working with at a scientific level — not just as rows and columns, but as physical systems, regulatory frameworks, and real-world processes. That context makes the models I build more accurate and the insights more actionable.

I hold an LPDP scholarship (Indonesia's most competitive government scholarship) and professional certifications in hazardous waste management, ISO auditing, and mining operations.

I'm based in Jakarta but open to opportunities globally and remotely — wherever environmental expertise and data science create the most value together.
